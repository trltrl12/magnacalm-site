FAQ

Common questions.

Glycinate is chelated — bonded to the amino acid glycine — which dramatically improves absorption and bypasses the GI issues common with oxide or citrate forms. It crosses the blood-brain barrier more effectively and doesn't cause the laxative effect many experience with cheap magnesium.

KSM-66® is a patented, full-spectrum ashwagandha root extract from Ixoreal Biomed. It's the only ashwagandha extract produced using milk as an extraction solvent (traditional Ayurvedic method), standardized to ≥5% withanolides, and backed by 24+ human clinical trials. Generic "ashwagandha" extracts often have no standardization, no trials, and unverified potency.

Both ingredients are generally considered safe alongside most contraceptives. As always, we recommend consulting your doctor — particularly if you're on thyroid medication, as ashwagandha can influence thyroid hormone levels.

Many customers notice better sleep quality within the first 3–7 days. The cortisol-lowering and stress-resilience benefits of KSM-66® typically build over 4–6 weeks of consistent daily use, which is consistent with the clinical trial timelines.
