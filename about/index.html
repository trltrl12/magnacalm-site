About Us

Plants, science, and
transparency.

We started Terra Botanics because we were tired of supplement brands that hide behind proprietary blends and mystery ingredients. We believe you deserve to know exactly what you're putting in your body — and that it should actually work.

Our Mission

To create supplements that combine the wisdom of traditional botanicals with modern clinical research. Every ingredient we use is backed by published studies, sourced transparently, and dosed at effective levels — not token amounts designed to look good on a label.

What We Believe

We believe what you leave out of a supplement matters just as much as what you put in. That's why you'll never find artificial fillers, synthetic colors, or unnecessary additives in our products. Just clean, effective formulations your body can actually use.

Every batch is third-party tested for purity and potency. Our sourcing is fully transparent. And our formulas are designed by people who actually take them every day.

Why MagnaCalm?

MagnaCalm was our first product because we saw a gap: most magnesium supplements use cheap, poorly absorbed forms, and most ashwagandha products use generic extracts with inconsistent potency. We combined the best forms of both — Magnesium Glycinate and KSM-66 Ashwagandha — into a single daily capsule that actually delivers results.
